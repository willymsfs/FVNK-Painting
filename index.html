A Guide to Flawless Crack Repair

From hairline fractures to structural faults, we use specialized materials to restore integrity.

Conquering Water Seepage: The Permanent Solution

Painting a damp wall is a recipe for disaster. We solve the problem at its source.

  1. Find the Source: We conduct a thorough investigation of the terrace, exterior walls, and plumbing to find the root cause of the leak.
  2. Fix the Source: We perform crucial repairs, from applying new waterproofing membranes on the terrace to fixing exterior cracks.
  3. Wall Restoration: We remove damaged plaster, allow the wall to dry completely, and apply a cementitious waterproof barrier.
  4. Replaster & Repaint: Only after the wall is fully protected and dry do we replaster and begin our signature painting process.
